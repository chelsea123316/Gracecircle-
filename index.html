Welcome to GraceCircle

GraceCircle is a Christ-centered global community of women inspiring one another to grow in faith, purpose, and strength. We believe in living out God’s love through action — supporting one another, sharing stories of hope, and building a worldwide sisterhood where every woman feels seen, heard, and empowered.

Our History

Grace Circle Sisters Organization was founded on September 24th, 2021, by two sisters, Chelsea Chiamaka Iroham and Sofia Linda Iroham, who shared a passion for uplifting women and spreading God’s love. It was officially launched on September 21st, 2025.

Vision

To create a world where every woman, no matter her background or location, is empowered to rise, shine, and walk boldly in her God-given purpose.

Mission Statement

GraceCircle is dedicated to providing women with spiritual guidance, encouragement, and empowerment opportunities. Through mentorship, skill development, and Christ-centered outreach, we help women overcome challenges, grow in confidence, and live out their divine calling.

🌿 Our Core Values

  • 💖 Faith and Purpose — living guided by God’s plan
  • 🌸 Compassion and Service — showing love through action
  • 🤝 Unity and Sisterhood — building a global family of faith
  • 🌼 Growth through Empowerment — helping every woman rise
  • 🔥 Integrity and Action — walking boldly in truth and purpose

Programs & Initiatives

GraceCircle offers mentorship and leadership programs, financial literacy and self-growth workshops, support groups, spiritual retreats, and volunteer empowerment activities — all rooted in faith and compassion.
